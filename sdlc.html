SDLC

Software Development Life Cycle is a process that produces software ensuring high quality and low time cost.

  1. Planning & requirement analysis
  2. Defining requirements
  3. Design/architecture
  4. Building the product
  5. Testing
  6. Deployment & maintenance

Requirement analysis is the most important and fundamental stage. It is performed by the senior members of the team with inputs from the customer, the sales department, market surveys and domain experts in the industry.
